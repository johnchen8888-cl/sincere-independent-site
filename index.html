Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our project supply and one-stop solutions.

What is SINCERE Building Solutions?

SINCERE is a professional supplier of one-stop interior building materials, specializing in custom cabinets, interior doors, and millwork for global residential and commercial projects.

Do you provide free design services?

Yes, we provide free layout suggestions and CAD drawing support for projects. Just send us your floor plans or project dimensions.

What products are included in your one-stop package?

Our package includes interior doors, kitchen cabinets, bathroom vanities, closets, wall panels, and smart locks, ensuring finish consistency across the project.

Do you support OEM and private label for dealers?

Yes, we offer comprehensive OEM and private label support for dealers, contractors, and global buyers looking to build their own brand presence.
