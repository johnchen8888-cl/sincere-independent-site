Project Overview

For this premium kitchen cabinet system, the developer required high-durability surfaces without sacrificing luxury aesthetics. We selected anti-fingerprint PET laminate for the cabinet fronts and paired them with 20mm grade-A quartz countertops for maximum heat and scratch resistance.

The main construction challenge was the integration of a custom waterfall island with concealed plumbing and electrical conduits. By providing detailed shop drawings and technical specification support during the design phase, SINCERE ensured that the prefabricated cabinetry was installation-ready upon arrival. This professional coordination significantly reduced on-site labor costs and improved project efficiency for the contractor. Our solution also included a matched interior door and millwork package to maintain a unified design language throughout the open-plan residential project.
