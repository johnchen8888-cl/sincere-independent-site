Master Bedroom

Details & Craftsmanship

In this high-end residential project, material selection was critical to achieving finish consistency across the master suite. We utilized premium walnut wood veneer for the custom wardrobe modules and matched it with 45mm solid core interior doors for enhanced acoustic insulation and a heavy, luxury feel.

A primary construction challenge involved the seamless integration of ceiling-height wardrobe panels with the surrounding architectural wall cladding system. To resolve this, SINCERE implemented an integrated millwork approach, utilizing prefabricated sub-frames and precision CAD-guided installation to ensure zero-gap alignment between the door frames and wall panels. This project demonstrates our capability in coordinating multi-category interior packages—including custom millwork and project-ready interior door systems—while maintaining strict quality control over material transitions and surface durability.
